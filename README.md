# Goose War 
A real-time strategy game where you control tanks, capture territory, and conquer your enemies. Built with HTML5 Canvas and vanilla JS.

## Current Features

- **Command your army** – Select units with left click, send them anywhere with right click
- **Capture territory** – Units automatically claim nearby tiles for your faction
- **Strategic combat** – Tanks face enemies to attack (direction matters!)
- **Amphibious ops** – Transporter ships carry land units across water
- **Full camera control** – Drag to pan, scroll to zoom in/out
- **Multiple factions** – Green (you) vs Red (AI enemies)

## How to Play

1. **Left click** a green unit → selects it  
2. **Left click** anywhere → unit moves there  
3. **Right click** anywhere → unit rotates to face that direction  
4. **Drag mouse** → pan camera  
5. **Scroll wheel** → zoom in/out  

**Pro tip:** Use transporters to sneak land units across rivers and flank the enemy!
